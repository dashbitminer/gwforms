CHILD SAFEGUARDING AND PROTECTION

Glasswing International is dedicated to providing dignified care and safe environments for girls, boys, and adolescents, as well as their families, whenever they interact with our organization. We recognize the importance of ensuring due process in cases of child abuse, promptly addressing and resolving such incidents without tolerance for negligence or inattention. In alignment with this commitment, Glasswing maintains a Zero Tolerance Declaration against child abuse and mistreatment. All volunteers must commit and adhere to this declaration, irrespective of the program, project, or activity they are involved in, or the duration of their volunteer service.

ZERO TOLERANCE STATEMENT AGAINST CHILD VIOLENCE, ABUSE, AND MISTREATMENT

At Glasswing International, safeguarding and protecting children and adolescents is paramount. We maintain an unwavering commitment to zero tolerance against all forms of violence, abuse (physical, emotional, and sexual), and mistreatment directed towards them. To uphold this commitment, Glasswing International has established a comprehensive Child Safeguarding and Protection Policy along with a Protocol for Care and Response for the Safeguarding and Protection of Children and Adolescents. It is the responsibility of Glasswing, its directors, and collaborators to ensure the strict implementation of these policies. Zero Tolerance means that any collaborator involved in an incident of violence, abuse (physical, emotional, and sexual), or mistreatment of children and adolescents, or who becomes aware of such an incident and fails to report it, will be immediately separated from the organization and reported to the appropriate authorities.

This policy applies to all collaborators and departments, whether directly or subcontracted, with permanent or temporary contracts, volunteers, and all types of suppliers, partners, and/or visitors (regardless of whether they have direct contact with children and adolescents) who are involved in programs and projects in all countries where Glasswing operates.
